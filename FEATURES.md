## For Students

### 🎯 Intelligent Diagnostic Loop
The foundation of adaptive learning: Bayesian Knowledge Tracing estimates your mastery of each grammar concept from every graded attempt across the platform (practice, writing, conversation).

**What to notice when exploring:**
- The **"Focus Areas"** dashboard prescribes exactly which concepts you need to review
- **Mastery curves** show your estimated confidence on each grammar topic in real time
- When mastery stays low (e.g., subjunctive vs. indicative), the system auto-surfaces the tutorial and targeted practice activity to close the gap

**Try it:** [Demo Link](https://concordancelearn.com/demo) → Complete 3-4 practice exercises → View the **Dashboard** to see how mastery updates

---

### 📚 Adaptive Practice
Multiple drill types calibrated to your proficiency level (Novice → Intermediate → Advanced), not just your grade level.

**Available activities:**
- **Conjugaison** — Verb conjugation drills with morphological feedback
- **Vocabulaire** — Spaced-repetition vocabulary in context
- **Compréhension** — Reading passages with inline glossaries tailored to your gaps
- **Connecteurs** — Logical connectors & discourse markers
- **Pronoms** — Relative pronouns & object complements
- **Concordance des temps** — Sequence of tenses & subjunctive conditioning
- And more grammar concepts

**What to notice:**
- Difficulty adjusts *within* each exercise based on your responses
- Vocabulary is scaffolded—uncommon words get glosses; familiar ones don't
- Every wrong answer surfaces the morphological/syntactic rule, immediately

**Try it:** [Demo Link](https://concordancelearn.com/demo) → Practice page → Try 2-3 different activity types

### 🤖 AI-Powered Conversation Partner
A French conversation partner calibrated to your current ACTFL proficiency level, providing *i+1* (comprehensible input just above your level). Real-time error tagging feeds straight back into your diagnostic loop.

**Features:**
- **Proficiency-aware scaffolding** — The AI speaks *to* your level, not at everyone's
- **Real-time error tags** — Mistakes are labeled (verb tense, gender agreement, etc.) as you go
- **Dual-provider fallback** — Backed by Google Gemini → Anthropic Claude fallback so outages don't kill the feature
- **Error loop closure** — Every tagged error feeds into your concept mastery model

**What to notice:**
- How the AI responds differently depending on your proficiency
- The error tags at the end of the conversation
- How conversational mistakes map to your **Focus Areas** dashboard

**Try it:** [Demo Link](https://concordancelearn.com/demo) → Conversation Partner → Have a 5-10 minute dialogue → Review the error tagging

### ✍️ Writing Diagnostics & AAPPL Exam Prep
Three writing tools aligned to real proficiency frameworks:

#### 1. **Generalized Writing Diagnostic**
Submit any piece of French writing (not just essays) and get AI feedback calibrated to your ACTFL sublevel (Novice High through Advanced).

#### 2. **Interactive Proofreading**
Morphosyntactic error tags (verb conjugation, tense sequencing, agreement) anchored directly to your submitted text, feeding straight into your mastery model.

#### 3. **AAPPL Expression Task Simulator**
Practice for the official AAPPL exam with a scoring rubric, practice simulator, and targeted word bank.

**What to notice:**
- How error feedback changes based on your proficiency
- The rubric alignment with official AAPPL standards

## Privacy & Compliance

Concordance is designed to be fully compliant with major educational privacy laws:

- **FERPA (US)** — Student education records are protected; teachers and admins control data
- **COPPA (US)** — Students under 13 can't sign up independently; relies on School Consent Exception
- **PPRA (US)** — No surveys on protected sensitive topics
- **GDPR (EU)** — Explicit consent required; self-service account deletion
